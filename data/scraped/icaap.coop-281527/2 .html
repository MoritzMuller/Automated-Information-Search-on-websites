Coop Colloquium 4.0

 

The ICA-AP Committee on Cooperative Research (CCR) is back with the 4th iteration of COOP Colloquium to be held on September 29 on the theme 'Cooperative Identity & Platform Technology'. It will be a starting point to dive deep into the influence of platform technology on cooperative identity, bringing together cooperative advocates, practitioners and young scholars from the Asia and Pacific region.

Register here.

 

 
Worker Cooperatives and Trade Unions: Exploring Relations in the Asia-Pacific Region

CICOPA invites you to join its upcoming webinar in collaboration with CICOPA Asia-Pacific on 28 September 2022. The experts from four countries: India, Japan, Australia, and the Philippines will share their experiences and explore how worker cooperatives and trade unions could cooperate with each other to protect workers’ rights (especially in vulnerable situations) and to empower them. For detailed program and registration details, click here.
Survey on Cooperative Identity

ICA had launched a survey to gather first thoughts from cooperators worldwide on how well the Statement on the Cooperative Identity is defined and understood. The participation of the global cooperative community in this consultation to deepen that identity is vital. Cooperatives everywhere in the world, their members and employees, and all other stakeholders are invited to complete this survey which is now available in 10 languages. Read More.
Global Innovation Coop Summit

Registrations are open for the Global Innovation Coop Summit to be held from September 26–27, 2022 from 9 am to 5 pm local time in Crédit Agricole, Paris (Hybrid). The Summit has been designed to create a dedicated space for cooperative leaders from all sectors to share, exchange, learn and build together a more inclusive plural economy. ICA-AP members will get a discount on the registration fee by using the coupon code ICAA-P10 while registering. Register Here.
