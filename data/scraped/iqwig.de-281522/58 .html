Welcome!

Here you can register as an external expert in our database. If you already have access, you can edit your existing data via the login.

Please note: Participation in the preparation of our assessments and generally understandable health information is only possible if you can provide your counselling services in German. Against this background, the form for registration as an external expert is also only available in German. We ask for your understanding.
