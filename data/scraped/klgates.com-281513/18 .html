Welcome to the global K&L Gates Alumni Network

We greatly value the contributions of our alumni to the firm, our clients, and our communities. We also understand the power of relationships and connectivity, which is why we've created the global K&L Gates Alumni Network. All K&L Gates alumni are encouraged to join the network to:

 
  • Create and renew trusted personal and professional connections with former colleagues and friends
  • Discover and register for K&L Gates events, including CLE seminars and programming for alumni only
  • Learn about job opportunities at the firm as well as those posted by members of the network
  • Collaborate and share ideas on topics of mutual interest with other alumni
  • Stay current on firm developments and news
