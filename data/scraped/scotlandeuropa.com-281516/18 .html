Scotland House Brussels

Scotland has had a long-established presence in Brussels, including 20 years in Scotland House Brussels at Rond-Point Schuman. In line with the Scottish Government’s newly-established network across the EU in London, Dublin, Berlin, and Paris, we have renewed our own Scotland House Brussels to provide us with an even more effective platform to enhance our commitment to playing an active role in Europe and support our collaborative approach to working with Scottish, EU and International stakeholders.

The partners of Scotland House Brussels are the Scottish Government, Scotland Europa and Scottish Enterprise/Scottish Development International. Scotland House Brussels has well established teams covering diplomatic engagement, economic development and cultural promotion.

Conference Centre

For the organisation of your meetings and events, Scotland House Brussels offers a friendly and cosy venue in the heart of the EU district. We have modern facilities for seminars, training workshops, conferences, meetings and receptions.

Scotland House Brussels Residents

Other than being the home of Scotland Europa and the Scottish Government EU office, Scotland House provides a base in Brussels for our Scottish members and partners involved in our activities, some of whom are permanently based here as Resident Members. We also have a number of non-Scottish Residents – other organisations who share similar interests to our own – such as other Regional representations to the EU, or EU level networks or companies operating in our fields of interest.

We offer our Residents a unique membership package, including office space, conference centre facilities and the bringing together of a wide variety of public and private sector organisations.

If you are interested in having more information about the office space and the services that we offer, contact Sarah Anhorn – E sarah.anhorn@scotent.co.uk / T +32 2 282 83 01

Scotland House Brussels Members

Since the beginning of 2019, we are pleased to be able to offer a new type of membership package for partners to utilise our co-working space at the heart of the Brussels European Quarter. As Scotland House Brussels Members, occasional visitors to Brussels can benefit from an ideal touch down area between EU institutional or project meetings, or to host them as well as network with Scotland House Brussels staff and other visiting partners and participate in its political, economic and cultural events throughout the year.

How to reach us

Scotland House Brussels is centrally located in Brussels’ European Quarter at Rond-Point Schuman 6, 1040 Brussels. It enjoys excellent transport links as Schuman metro and train stations are less than a few minutes’ walk and Brussels Airport is only 15 minutes by train or 25 minutes by bus or car.

You can also reach us at scotland.house@scotent.co.uk or +32 2 282 83 04.
