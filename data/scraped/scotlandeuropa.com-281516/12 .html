Conference Centre

1

We have adapted the capacity of our Conference Centre and meeting rooms to be in line with the guidance of Belgian Government to prevent the spread of COVID-19. We have implemented measures and protocols in order to ensure a safe environment for our visitors and our staff which is our priority. Please contact us to discuss your need and how we can accommodate your event in the best sanitary and healthy conditions possible.

Information

The Scotland House Brussels Conference Centre, located at the heart of the European Quarter, is the ideal location for organising meetings, workshops, seminars and even networking receptions. With our 3 modular rooms we can host up to 130 guests.

Our services include:

  • State-of-the-art audio-visual equipment
  • Natural light in all rooms
  • Free Wi-Fi internet access
  • Air conditioning
  • Conference Centre Coordinator support leading to and on the day of your event

For additional information or to plan your event, please contact Helena – E Helena.DeRaedemaeker@scotent.co.uk / T +32 2 282 83 15
