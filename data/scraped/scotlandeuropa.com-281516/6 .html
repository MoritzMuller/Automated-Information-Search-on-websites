Our partners

In addition the to the Scotland Europa Members , Scotland House Members and Scotland House Residents, Scotland Europa works closely with a number of Scottish partners in relation to European engagement.

 Scottish Government - The Scottish Government EU Office is located with Scotland Europa in Scotland House Brussels. To find out more about the Government’s EU priorities, consult Scotland’s Action Plan for EU Engagement
Enterprise Europe Scotland - EES is hosted by Scottish Enterprise and is a gateway to information on doing business and finding partners in Europe
European Parliament Information Office in Edinburgh - The office works to help increase awareness of the Parliament and the impact of its activities in Scotland, as well as highlighting the work of the six Scottish Members of the European Parliament (MEPs)

Scotland Europa also supports and helps coordinate our members’ engagement in a number of thematic networks in Brussels and at EU level. Read a more comprehensive list of our and our members’ collective networks.
