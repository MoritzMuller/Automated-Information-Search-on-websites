Scotland Europa Members' Meeting - MEMBERS ONLY

Scotland Europa
Membership

After two years online, Scotland Europa is excited to invite you all to our first in-person Members’ Meeting since March 2020. The event will take place on Tuesday 4th October, and Historic Environment Scotland have kindly offered to host us in their venue, the Engine Shed (The Engine Shed, Forthside Way, Stirling FK8 1QZ). The meeting will take place from 10am-4pm.

A lot has changed over the past few years, and this has influenced the way we work. The meeting will be a chance for Scotland Europa and our members to reconnect, to provide an opportunity to reflect on 30 years of networking Scotland in Europe, and to envision our future work.

You’ll get the opportunity to reconnect, reflect and reshape what successful engagement looks like today. As well drawing on insights from speakers and panellists throughout the day, we also want to hear about your experiences, challenges and ambitions. We’ll use interactive discussion sessions to explore together how we continue to work to our joint strengths, how best to adapt to our changing circumstances, and shape new opportunities for Scotland’s engagement in Europe.

Finally, to wrap it all up, we’d love to invite you to a thank you celebration for our 30th anniversary to reflect on all the work we’ve done and the milestones we’ve reached together.

Please note that you if you would like to attend both the Members' Meeting and the Thank You Celebration, you must select a ticket for each when registering. Otherwise, if you only intend to attend one of these events, then please select the relevant ticket.

You can register for the meeting here. If you have any issues with registration, please contact Lucie.McAnea@scotent.co.uk.

We can't wait to see everyone!
