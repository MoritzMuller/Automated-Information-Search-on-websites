Conference Centre (COVID-19)

As lockdown measures in Belgium are continuing to ease, Scotland House Brussels is beginning to re-open, and so there is once again the possibility to host your meetings at our facilities. Due to the ongoing crisis we have adapted our services to help to keep both you and our staff safe and allow your meeting to take place in a safe environment. The measures implemented by Scotland House Brussels are in line with the measures advised by the Belgian government to prevent the spread of COVID-19.

The updated use of the Conference Centre is in place for the summer and will evolve if necessary, in accordance with any updates to the government guidance on holding conferences.  This plan can be revised at any time if the situation requires. The below prices/room capacity apply until August 31, 2020, after which the normal prices/room capacity should be once again be applicable.

Our services include:

  • State-of-the-art audio-visual equipment
  • Natural light in all rooms
  • Free Wi-Fi internet access
  • Air conditioning
  • Conference Centre Coordinator support leading to and on the day of your event
