Membership

Scotland Europa provides a hub for Scottish organisations who aim for a stronger, strategic positioning in European affairs. Our team of EU policy and funding experts offer insights into EU public affairs, bringing careful analysis and fresh interpretation to European issues affecting Scottish interests. We work with our members, the Scottish Government and wider stakeholders to raise Scotlandâ€™s profile in the EU and to increase the level of expertise and knowledge of European issues within Scotland.

We offer tailor-made membership packages, depending on the type of support needed with services including:

  • EU funding and policy development
  • EU project support
  • Learning and capacity building
  • Networking and positioning

“Scotland Europa is an invaluable resource for both me personally and the wider university. We really couldn’t do our work with the European Commission and our European partners as well as we do without Scotland Europa’s assistance.”

Alan Kennedy, University of Edinburgh

With members drawn from Scottish organisations in the public, private, academic and third sectors, Scotland Europa’s partnership with European institutions gives us an influential voice and distinct insight into European affairs.
