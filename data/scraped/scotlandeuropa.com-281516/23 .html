Changemakers and Pioneers

Glasgow Caledonian University & University of the West of Scotland
Research & Innovation

Hosted by Glasgow Caledonian University and the University of the West of Scotland, this event is a celebration of the changemakers, the innovators and pioneers who have been able to develop unique projects through European funding. Despite the obstacles placed before us and the challenges for future collaboration there is a real need for us to continue to work together to ensure that our achievements have meaningful impact.
