POST LPRA RADIO SOLUTIONS CONFERENCE 2019 SUMMARY - ANOTHER GREAT SUCCESS! 

 

 

The LPRA Council would like to say a big thank you to all of the Speakers and Delgates for helping to make RS2019 such a great succes, and a special huge thank you to the event's host sponsors, LPRA Member Nedap N.V. 

The statistics collated from the Post-Conference Delegate Survey speak for themselves :

Average overall score for RS2019 : 8.8 out of 10

96.87% of Delegates and Speakers would attend a future LPRA Conference

Average score for RS2019 Speakers : 4.24 out of 5

31 Companies / Institutions were represented at RS2019

14 Speakers gave 15 Presentations

The speakers from RS2019 have very generously agreed to share their presentations with LPRA members and those who were unable to attend the Conference*. 14 of the 15 PowerPoint presentations can be found on this LINK HERE the Copyright remains with each individual speaker.


The feedback from the Post RS2019 Conference Survey will be used to ensure that RS2021 will be an even bigger and better event. 

To be kept fully informed of the LPRA's next Radio Solutions Conference (RS2021), please contact the LPRA Secretariat HERE
