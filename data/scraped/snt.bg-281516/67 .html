S&T Bulgaria participates as an exhibitor at the 1st National Congress of Angiology

15.05.2021 | Bulgaria

On May 14 and 15, 2021, the first congress of the newly established Bulgarian Society of Angiology was held at the Hilton Sofia Hotel. The event has been postponed since 2020 due to the Covid 19 pandemic

In the two-day forum, specialists from various specialties got acquainted with the latest developments in the diagnosis, drug and endovascular treatment of vascular diseases. A special guest and speaker at the congress was Dr. M. Lichtenberg, President of the German Society of Angiology (DGA).

The 2021 Angiology Summit included a demonstration of diagnostic procedures, various endovascular techniques and lectures on peripheral vascular interventions. The foundations have been laid for an annual congress on Angiology, which will be a trademark for a forum with an extremely high level of participation, demonstration of world-established practices and trends in the treatment of peripheral vascular diseases.

Professionals in interventional cardiology and vascular surgery had the opportunity to visit the exhibition stand of S&T Bulgaria and to get acquainted with Philips Healthcare angiography systems.

Philips is the leader in image-guided therapy, providing integrated solutions that advance minimally invasive procedures.
