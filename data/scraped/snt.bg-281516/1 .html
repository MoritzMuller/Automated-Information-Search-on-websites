latest angiographic technology

The latest angiographic technology is in the most high-tech center for cerebrovascular diseases in the University Hospital "St. Ivan Rilski ”

The equipment Azurion 7 Biplane, delivered and installed by “S&T Bulgaria”, is the latest generation of angiographic devices produced by Philips. It was created in collaboration with the world's leading hospitals and is an innovative platform that allows professionals to easily and confidently perform a wide range of procedures - both routine and highly complex.

the National Congress of Ultrasound in Medicine

S&T Bulgaria took part in the 23rd edition of the National Congress of Ultrasound in Medicine

This is the largest multidisciplinary medical congress in the country and consists of two parts - School of Ultrasound and a real part of the congress with scientific sessions. The program includes cardiology, angiology, pulmonology, nephrology, endocrinology, gastroenterology, COVID-19, pediatrics and numerous satellite symposia.
