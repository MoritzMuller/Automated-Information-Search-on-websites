Member Benefits

Membership of SHFCA has grown organically since our formation in March 2004 and currently stands at over 170 member companies and organisations, plus over 35 reciprocal membership organisations who are associate members. 

The following are just some of the benefits you will enjoy as a SHFCA Member.

  • Be part of an exciting and growing industry sector and have a say in the way our industry develops

  • Be part of a single, coherent body networked into UK and overseas organisations including the Australian Hydrogen Council, BCGA, Canadian Hydrogen & Fuel Cell Association, Hydrogen Bavaria, Hydrogen Denmark, HySafe, IGEM, and the UK Hydrogen and Fuel Cell Association.

  • Participate in collective marketing and promotional opportunities

  • Opportunities to exhibit or speak at the events in which SHFCA participates, including the annual exhibition and conference at All-Energy in Glasgow, and our SHFCA Annual Conference

  • Through our partnership with Scottish Development International (SDI), enjoy access to support for participation in international events. Applies to Scottish organisations only.

  • Enjoy free listing on the SHFCA multi-functional website (www.shfca.org.uk) linked to your own website.

  • Benefit from active support by the SHFCA team to represent members' interests at all levels.

  • Take part in regular Members networking meetings and webinars to learn what others are doing, and take advantage of the opportunity to interact with other members and experts from around the world.
