The Scottish Hydrogen and Fuel Cell Association (SHFCA) promotes and develops Scottish expertise in fuel cells and hydrogen technologies.

SHFCA brings together Scotland’s specialised fuel cell companies, power generation companies, academic institutions, research and development bodies, energy consultants, Scottish Enterprise and local enterprise companies and councils with an interest in Hydrogen & Fuel Cells. Membership is open to all-comers.

HTP4 webinar Commissioning & Operations
4 Oct 2022

You are invited to join the fourth HTP webinar on Tuesday 4th October from 14:00-15:15 BST (15:00–16:15 CET) on Hydrogen Valley project Commissioning, Training & Operations.

This HTP webinar will cover key aspects of commissioning and operations of Hydrogen Valley / Hydrogen Island projects. This event will provide insights about developing real-world hydrogen projects, based on practical experience from the BIG HIT project activities on Orkney.

Hydrogen Strategies in the North Sea Region
11 Oct 2022

This one day conference, with keynote speakers from UK and Scottish governments, will look at the important role that logistics and the maritime sector will play in establishing hydrogen production and trade across the North Sea. Invited speakers will outline the development of national hydrogen strategies and present maritime hydrogen projects, and the day concludes with a discussion on the need for collaboration in research and innovation for the creation of a North Sea hydrogen economy.

SHFCA2022 Hydrogen Scotland conference
20 Oct 2022

Our 14th SHFCA2022 annual conference takes place on Wednesday 20th October 2022 as an in-person event at the prestigious McEwan Hall in Edinburgh. The Hydrogen Scotland programme brings together SHFCA members and guests with invited expert speakers who will address the importance of building partnerships and sharing lessons learned as we scale up production of hydrogen to deliver Scotland’s target of 5GW low carbon hydrogen production by 2030.
