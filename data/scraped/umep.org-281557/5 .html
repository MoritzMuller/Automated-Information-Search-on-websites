Creating the environment likely to attract and ensure the safe
and optimized passage of goods through LE HAVRE
Union Maritime et Portuaire du HAVRE is a maritime and port federation which federate professional organizations of the Port of LE HAVRE. It’s a direct link between members of the Port Community, a tool at the disposal of its clients – especially regarding professional education – and a driving force for the economy of the harbour
