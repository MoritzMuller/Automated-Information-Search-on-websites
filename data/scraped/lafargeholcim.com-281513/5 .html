Life at Holcim


Building progress for people and the planet is a bold ambition. We believe that only by working together in an inclusive environment where everyone can grow and thrive, can we build the world we all want to live in.

At Holcim, you’ll be part of a group of 70,000 passionate, pioneering and high-performing people. Our teams inspire each other to deliver the most innovative and sustainable building solutions to accelerate the world’s green transformation.

We believe everyone contributes something unique. Whether that’s your commitment to sustainability and innovation, your belief in the power of collaboration and diversity, or your passion for learning.

We foster a culture of collaboration that supports your development with world-class learning programs and will challenge you to bring your best every day. At Holcim, you can put your passion to work.

What we do


Introducing Holcim

As a global leader in innovative and sustainable building solutions, Holcim is enabling greener cities, smarter infrastructure and improving living standards around the world. With sustainability at the core of our strategy, we are becoming a net-zero company, with our people and communities at the heart of our success. We are driving circular construction as a world leader in recycling, to build more with less. Our offer focuses on four core areas: Cement, Ready-Mix Concrete, Aggregates and Solutions & Products. Our business is made possible by our 70,000 talented people around the world who are passionate about building progress for people and the planet.

Why it matters


Building the world we want to live in

Our world is growing, with the global population expected to reach 10 billion by 2050. Rapid urbanization means that 70% of people are expected to live in cities by the same date. With 1.6 billion people lacking adequate housing and sanitation, we need to build liveable cities that work for all. At Holcim, we are part of the solution. And you can be too. Be part of our vision to become the global leader in innovative and sustainable building solutions. Together, we have an important role to play in accelerating our world’s transition toward a net-zero future. Build progress with us.

Making cities greener from foundation to rooftop, from our low-carbon materials to our energy-efficiency and renovation solutions
Empowering society with smarter infrastructure, with tailored systems to enable more green mobility, renewable energy and essential sanitation
Improving living standards for all, increasing access to affordable housing with innovative partnerships and technologies
