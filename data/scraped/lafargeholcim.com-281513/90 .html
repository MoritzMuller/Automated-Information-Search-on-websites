BUILD YOUR GREEN CITY WITH US

Building for people and the planet, we offer sustainable building materials for any type of project, to enable green cities around the world. With one-third of net sales already in green building solutions, we make it easier to make the green choice with our EcoLabel, transparently sharing the environmental benefits of our products, from low carbon to recycled content. From houses to transport and energy, discover some of our smart solutions to build your green city.

Roads

Sustainable road-building is essential to building the circulatory system of the green city. Pedestrians, cyclists and roads should all enjoy equal access to safe, durable and eco-friendly pathways for work and pleasure. With infrastructure, there’s no such thing as “one size fits all.” 

That’s especially true of roads, which vary widely depending on location, climate, vehicles, and cities. The nature of the soil can have a major influence on the overall road project. We are experts in soil stabilization and can offer solutions that suit any conditions. LafargeHolcim can help meet any challenge, anywhere.

Discover our green building solutions

To make road projects sustainable you can start with the assessment of the best solutions and materials to build roads in your area. Adding the power of digital, our ORIS platform for road design optimization helps reduce road project costs by up to one-third and carbon footprint by up to half while tripling road durability and lifespan.

The road pavement must be durable to ensure long lifespan and low maintenance of road surfaces - hence less CO2 emissions. On the top of this, the use of recycled aggregates can strongly minimize the environmental impact of road projects. For example, aggneo is our range of high-quality aggregates processed from recycled concrete and thus help meet the challenges of construction and demolition within a circular economy.

Highways

Our integrated offer for road projects includes a complete range of products and services, best-in-class site supply management, and expertise in project management. We leverage a unique solutions portfolio that encompasses: soil stabilization, aggregates layers, concrete pavements, asphalt formulations, roller-compacted concrete, and fast-hardening concrete.

Traffic roads

Soil stabilization is an effective way to reduce CO2 emissions in road construction. Hydraulic binders like Doroport and Dorosol stabilize the ground and prepare it for the project. For a net zero road pavement, Green City used SuperLow, the industry’s first carbon-neutral asphalt. It is manufactured at reduced temperatures and the remaining emissions are offset through climate projects. SuperLow can cool quicker, meaning less time for road repairs.

Port

Ports are the critical interface between marine and land transport. Just as the airport is the hub for cargo with a high cost-to-volume ratio, so ports are critical for their opposite -- such as raw materials for heavy industry. This method of transport generally lowers the carbon footprint of goods conveyed compared to land or air.

Discover our green building solutions

Ports normally require large volumes of aggregates and concrete. ECOPact, our range of green concrete, is ideal for such projects. ECOPact is sold at a range of low-carbon levels, from 30% to 100% less carbon emissions compared to standard concrete. Where regulatory conditions allow, ECOPact products integrate upcycled construction and demolition materials, further closing the resource loop.

Bridge

Bridges compress the green city’s footprint, allowing residents to live and work on multiple levels at the same time, with overlapping layers and traffic corridors. Bridges are also typically the arteries for millions of visitors and commuters to dash into the city. They must be completely reliable under a range of climatic conditions over a lifespan of decades.

Discover our green building solutions

Bridges normally require large volumes of aggregates and concrete. What better source for bridge-building in Green City than ECOPact, our range of green concrete that is sold at a range of low CO2 levels, from 30% to 100% less carbon emissions compared to standard concrete. Where allowed, ECOPact integrates upcycled construction and demolition materials, further closing the resource loop.

Whether looking to build a new bridge or rehabilitate an existing one, with Ductal® UHPC you can build more with less. From joint fill to connect precast elements to bridge deck overlays to ensure durability, or for pier jacketing to guarantee longevity and seismic resistance, Ductal® is ready to take on your next major challenge.

Dam

When designed correctly, dams provide green, renewable energy to Green City. They are also critically important to Green City’s overall water management strategy, storing water in times of excess water flow and releasing it when natural flows are inadequate to meet water demand.

Discover our green building solutions

Airport

Well-designed, well-managed airports are key to the vitality and economy of the green city. They are the emblem of openness to the global economy, channeling high-value, low-weight, time-sensitive cargo, which includes everything from tourists to semiconductors.

Discover our green building solutions

Airports are normally symbols of the green city’s arrival on the world stage and aesthetics are no small consideration. Ductal UHPC, offers unmatched qualities of durability, strength, aesthetics and ductility, while Artevia decorative concrete gives free rein to bold aesthetic and architectural projects. Both are also long-lasting, ensuring the airport’s long lifespan.

Extensia concrete extends industrial floors, helping to put buildings into service quicker and better resist wear and impact of heavy vehicle traffic and high mechanical loads. Expansion projects

Growth means more efficient and larger airports but upgrade and expansion projects are typically costly and lengthy. Not in Green City. Here we use Chronolia high early strength concrete to gain time (3-5 times faster than standard concrete), reduce cost and avoid close contact between workers on site. It’s perfect for projects on a tight schedule or which have experienced disruptions, and to improve quality in key airport surfaces such as the apron.

Housing

Sustainable urban living is a combination of many factors, beginning with the homes we live in and the materials we use to build them. We need to apply low-carbon and circular solutions wherever possible, lower the carbon and energy intensity of our homes over their lifecycle, and look to recycling the construction and demolition waste when they finally are replaced.

Discover our green building solutions

Susteno cement, the first and only resource-saving cement in Europe that uses fine mixed granulate from demolished buildings as an additive, is the perfect foundation for building green and circular housing in Green City. 

To insulate those homes, Airium mineral foam has a lower CO2 footprint over its lifecycle than traditional insulating products, on top of offering better air quality and higher energy savings.

Insulation helps keep interiors cool, thus reducing energy consumption from air conditioning and other cooling technology. Airium's special quality is that it can be used to insulate and build at the same time, saving both time and materials. This mineral insulating foam is fully fire-resistant and long-lasting, with the capacity to fill any type of shape and cavity - ideal for both building and renovation.

Walls

Walls can be built in different ways, and we have sustainable solutions for each of them. With Airium bricks you can build and insulate at the same time. Finally, special masonry cements such as Maestro or Forte reduce the carbon footprint and the amount of materials needed per m2, while offering faster and better workability.
