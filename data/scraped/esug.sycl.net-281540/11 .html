Current Projects of ESUG

After a study for UNEP Convention on Conservation of Migratory Species (CMS) in 2013, ESUG worked with CMS Raptors MOU, International Association for Falconry and Conservation of Birds of Prey (IAF) and BirdLife International to build portals to promote conservation through use of the Saker Falcon. The Final Report for Phase I of the project was recently released.

 

After completing the Sakernet portal for that project, ESUG was contracted by IAF to create in 2017 a Perdixnet system for restoring farmland biodiversity, with small game birds as a flagship. Agreement with IUCN's group for Sustainable Use and Management of Ecosystems is leading in 2019 to a global network for encouraging conservation through sustainable use.

 

 

 

 


ESUG was contracted to develop the Flagship Project for a Saker Falcon Portal and Network. The portal's back-office and construction site was based on design for www.naturalliance.eu. A live site is in Asian languages only. Networking has started through links to the Science and Admin site based on our System for Community Liaison.
