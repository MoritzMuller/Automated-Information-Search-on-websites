Invicta™ Sim Racing Boots

€101.64

Excl. VAT €84.00

To achieve the best performance and stay unconquered, you need the right equipment. Imagine, after hours of driving with your feet against aluminum pedal footplates, as you’ve smashed your brake to hit apex on every corner of Spa, your feet start cramping, and you lose grip of the pedal. That’s why you need sim racing boots!   

Invicta Sim Racing Boots are made with a thin rubber sole that gives you the perfect, stable grip you need to perform your best. The smooth, elegant boots with classic Asetek SimSports® design features are developed with full immersion and comfort in mind. The front has perforations to ensure maximum breathability, and the tongue is padded for extra comfort to make your sim racing experience effortless. The boots are equipped with a self-fastening strap and laces that provide easy adjustment and a perfect fit.
