THE SECRET OF THE SEA

“For more than a century, wind and sun have been and are our greatest allies in obtaining a totally natural sea salt”.

After an activity as apparently simple as the production of sea salt, a complex millennial task hides.
The balance between tradition, inherited from the Roman Empire, and permanent innovation in technology and knowledge development, plays a key role in guaranteeing the quality of our product.

THE LANDSCAPE OF LAS SALINAS

A natural landscape of more than 2500 hectares.

The sea salt basins of Bras del Port extend over more than 2,500 hectares, mostly occupied by lagoons where sea water is concentrated and salt is crystallized.
The salt exploitation, responsible for the conservation of the wetland, has created a habitat of exceptional ecological and landscape value.
