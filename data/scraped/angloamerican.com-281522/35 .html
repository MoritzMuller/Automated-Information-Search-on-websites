      Covid-19 resources

      At Anglo American, our main priority is the safety and health of our employees and our wider communities. As part of our group-wide response to COVID-19, we have designed a Community Response Plan (CRP) to guide site teams as to the initiatives that should be undertaken in order to provide the most effective and relevant support for their host communities.

      Drawing on the Social Way Management System, the CRP has been developed by engaging with our communities, traditional and faith leaders, and government agencies to understand their identified needs and ensure we provide the right support at the right time, both during the pandemic and into the vital economic recovery phase.

      We have produced guidance for our operations on how best to respond and have made this publicly available for other companies to use.
