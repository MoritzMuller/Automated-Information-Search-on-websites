      I WANT TO:

      Apply for a job

      You can find all group job-related information in our Careers section. If you need any further assistance, please read our Careers FAQs.

      Become a supplier

      You can find all group supplier information in our Suppliers section. If you need any further assistance, please visit Tools for suppliers.

      Enquire about shares

      You can find all share-related information in our Investors section. If you need any further assistance, please visit Shareholder contacts.

      Purchase our products

      You can find all product-related information in our Products section. If you need any further assistance, please email:salesandmarketingenquiries@angloamerican.com.

      Request a report

      If you would like to request a physical copy of the Annual Report, then please email CorporateAffairs@angloamerican.com. We no longer print physical copies of any of the other reports.
      Or please visit our dedicated Annual Reporting hub to find out more.
