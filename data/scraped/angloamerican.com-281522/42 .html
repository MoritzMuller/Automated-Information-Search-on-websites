Message from Chief Executive of Anglo American

YourVoice is Anglo American’s and De Beers Group’s confidential reporting service. It supports us to be the very best company that we can be.

YourVoice is available to 24hrs to all our employees, contractors, suppliers and other stakeholders, it enables them to raise concerns about potentially unethical, unlawful or unsafe conduct or practices that conflict with our Values and Code of Conduct, without fear of retaliation. It is an important tool in helping us to deliver our strategy and live our Purpose each and every day.

We want to know if you see things that trouble you about the way in which the organisation and its people do business. This is Your Voice and your opportunity to be heard. Any report you make will be kept confidential in accordance with Anglo American policies and applicable legislation protecting acts of disclosure.
