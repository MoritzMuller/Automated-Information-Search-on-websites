WBCSD COVID-19 Response Program

    WBCSD has initiated a special call to action for our members, leveraging our combined business expertise to address this crisis and support the critical role of business. Three new projects – vital supply chains, return to “normal” scenarios and long-term impacts – are underway with regular updates provided through our channels.
