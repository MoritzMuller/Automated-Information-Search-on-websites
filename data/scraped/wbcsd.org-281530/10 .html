    Peter Bakker is President and CEO of the World Business Council for Sustainable Development (WBCSD).

    Explore his latest thinking below in the collection of insights articles, and featured media articles and multimedia.

    Mr Bakker has led WBCSD since 2012. He is a distinguished business leader who, until June 2011, served as CFO and then CEO of TNT NV, the global transport and logistics company.
