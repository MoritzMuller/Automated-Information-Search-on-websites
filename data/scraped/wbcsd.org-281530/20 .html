Education

WBCSD places a strong emphasis on the need for education on sustainable development and business.

Education with regards to sustainable development is needed to provide a deeper understanding of the opportunities and challenges, the role of business, and to share learning on business solutions. We partner with leading universities such as Yale University, ESADE Business School and INSEAD to support our leading educational programs and tools.

    Highlights

    Leading Women and LEAP

    Now is the time for business to commit to SDG 5, which aims to achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls by 2030. Through its new LEAP project, WBCSD is aiming to accelerate this transition.

    Leadership Program

    A unique executive education program, focused on business, leadership and sustainable development.

    Collaboration

    Working collaboratively with partners is a crucial element in designing and scaling up business solutions.

    Customized programs

    We can develop customized educational programs for companies upon request.
