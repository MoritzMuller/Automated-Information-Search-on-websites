Careers

NEO GROUP offers a true career for its employees. We pay great attention to the professional and personal development of our people as we know that this contributes greatly to our competitive advantage.

Our investment in state-of-the-art production technology and infrastructure is well supported by our careful selection process of well-matched employees. With approximately 200 employees, NEO GROUP’s dedicated workforce includes many highly qualified specialists across a number of fields, including engineering, product development and project management.

We value highly skilled people that are willing to take initiative, take responsibility and be open to innovation. NEO GROUP is also keen to hear from recent graduates from technically-orientated subjects that can benefit from the in-house training opportunities we offer. Working with NEO GROUP delivers an interesting, challenging and rewarding career. NEO GROUP offers employees continuous progression, a competitive salary and an excellent working environment.
