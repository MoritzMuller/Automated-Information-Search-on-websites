Consultancy

Our market insights team is well-placed to provide bespoke and incisive insights on the global tin industry on a consultancy basis. Our knowledge and experience includes a deep understanding of the upstream and downstream tin industry both inside and outside of China as well as strong technical knowledge of existing and potential future new uses developed during the International Tin Association’s history as a major tin R&D institution.

We also offer Cost Modelling services for global mining operations. Developed using our industry insight, along with data from our producer members, the ITA Tin Mine Production Cost Model covers over 160 past, present and future mines from 2006 out to 2030.

Find out more about ITA's consultancy services

ITA’s consultancy services are the result of years of technical understanding, market analyst and close communications with our members and customers.
