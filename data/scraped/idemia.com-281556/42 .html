Governance

Our governance operate on the pillars of trust, performance, transparency, and strong ethical standards to guide our strategy and long-term decision making.

Driving IDEMIA’s growth strategy

At IDEMIA, our mission is to unlock the world and make it safer for people to navigate the physical and digital worlds in total security. Our corporate governance is guided by a shared commitment to maintaining trust, performance, transparency and strong ethical standards to shape our strategy and long-term decision making.
Our corporate governance framework is made up of our Supervisory Board, responsible for overseeing the management of the company and supervising the implementation of its strategy, and our Executive Committee, responsible for implementing the strategy set by the Board.

IDEMIA Supervisory Board

The Supervisory Board of IDEMIA Group supervises and controls the management of the Group. Composed of eight members and two observers, the Board supervises the implementation of the company’s strategy, taking account of the interests of all company stakeholders. The functioning of and decision-making within the Supervisory Board are governed by the company’s Articles of Association.

Yann Delabrière

Chairman of the Board

Yann Delabrière is a graduate of Ecole Normale Supérieure and Ecole Nationale d’Administration and has a postgraduate degree in mathematics. He began his career at the French Court of Audit (Cour des Comptes) before working for the French Foreign Trade Minister. He became Chief Financial Officer for the French export credit agency, COFACE, and then for Printemps Group, before joining PSA Peugeot Citroën as Chief Financial Officer in 1990, as well as Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of Banque PSA Finance in 1998. In 2007, Yann was appointed Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of Faurecia and served in that role until mid-2017. From June 2017 to February 2018, he chaired the Zodiac Aerospace executive board.

At the same time, Yann was a member of the Board of Directors of Cap Gemini from 2004 to May 2018, a member of the Board of Directors of Société Générale from 2012 to 2016, and has been Lead Director of Alstom since March 2017. He has also been a member of the Supervisory Board of STMicroelectronics since June 2020.

In February 2018, Yann was appointed Chairman of the Supervisory Board of IDEMIA. In October 2018, he became Chief Executive Officer of IDEMIA until June 2020, when he returned to the Chairmanship of the Group Supervisory Board.

Jean-François Cirelli

Member of the board

Since the end of 2015, Jean-François CIRELLI is the Chairman and Senior Adviser of the French, Belgian and Luxembourg subsidiaries of Blackrock.

Jean-François is also a senior advisor with Advent International and McKinsey & Company.

Before joining Blackrock, Jean-François was Vice President and Chief Executive Officer of GDF SUEZ (now known as ENGIE), where he was directly responsible for all energy activities in Europe between 2012 and 2014. Prior to its merger with Suez, Jean-François was Chairman and General Manager of Gaz de France, a position he held from 2004. Jean-François began his professional career in the French administration by joining the Treasury Department of the French Ministry of Economy and Finance in 1985. He was also Alternate Director for France at the IMF.

In 1995, Jean-François was appointed economic advisor to the French President where he remained until 2002. In 2002, he was appointed Deputy Director of the Cabinet of the Prime Minister Jean-Pierre RAFFARIN, in charge of economic, industrial and social matters.

Jean-François is a graduate of the Institut d’Etudes Politiques de Paris and holds a law degree. He is a former student of the Ecole Nationale d’Administration (1983-1985). Has been awarded Officier de la Légion d’Honneur and Officier du Mérite.

Pierre Barrial

President & CEO

Pierre BARRIAL is IDEMIA’s President & Chief Executive Officer.

Pierre has more than 25 years of experience and knowledge gained across multicultural and international environments in different industries (IT, Security), serving both the governmental and enterprise markets.

Pierre began his career at Bull Group where he held multiple sales and management functions between 1995 and 2006 to develop business in France and Africa. In 2006, he became Chief Executive Officer of Bull Italy, the second largest subsidiary of the Group. From July 2007 to June 2012, Pierre was Managing Director of the Central & Eastern Europe business, serving both government and enterprise sectors, where he successfully repositioned the offering on High value products, Solutions and Services. In August 2012, he became Director of Strategy, Development and Corporate Functions of Latin America and served as General Manager of Hispanic America.

Pierre Barrial joined Oberthur Technologies in 2013 as President of the Latin America region, before being appointed Executive Vice President of the Mobile Network Operator Business Unit. In January 2018, following the coming together of Oberthur Technologies and Morpho into the new IDEMIA Group, Pierre became the Executive Vice President of the Financial Institutions Business Unit, the largest activity of the Group.

In 2019, Pierre was appointed Chief Executive Officer of IDEMIA’s Secure Enterprise Transactions division, composed of IDEMIA’s transaction-based businesses (Financial institutions, Mobile operators, Automotive manufacturers…).

In July 2020, Pierre was appointed as the President & CEO of IDEMIA.

Born in 1972, Pierre BARRIAL graduated from the Paris School of Business in 1994, with a Bachelor in Business Administration.

Jean-Christophe Fondeur

Chief Technology Officer

Jean-Christophe Fondeur has been the Chief Technology Officer (CTO) of IDEMIA since the creation of the Group in 2017. He is responsible for IDEMIA’s central technology group, driving technological innovations with a specific focus on artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, biometrics, large-scale distributed systems, analytics solutions, cloud architectures and smart devices.

Under Jean-Christophe’s leadership, IDEMIA regularly confirms the robustness of its technologies, participating in U.S. National Institute of Standards & Technology (NIST) benchmark testing for iris, face, and fingerprint biometrics, always ranking in the top. He has also implemented several major institutional and academic partnerships for the Group to ensure that IDEMIA is always developing cutting-edge solutions to secure identity while guaranteeing private data privacy at all times.

Before IDEMIA, Jean-Christophe led Morpho’s research activities and was involved in the development of digital fingerprinting and face recognition algorithms, multi-biometric systems for the crossing of frontiers and scalable architecture design for very large biometric systems used by the FBI and UID (Unique Identification Authority) in India.

Jean-Christophe has created over a dozen patents and is a senior member of the IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronic Engineers). He holds engineering degrees from l’Ecole Polytechnique and Telecom ParisTech and is a frequent speaker at scientific conferences around the world.
