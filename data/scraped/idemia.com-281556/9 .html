Card issuance services

We unlock the issuer and cardholder experience with truly unique card issuance and card personalization services.

  • Payment

In the current transformation of the payment and banking industry the payment card—whether digital or physical, made of metal or recycled plastic—turns out to be the strongest link to the end customer, for traditional banks as well as for FinTechs. At IDEMIA, we strive to strengthen this link with a wide range of card issuance services and card personalization services.

We are the only global player in the market to master the complete payment card design and card issuance process—from the artwork creation to card personalization and delivery to the cardholder’s doorstep. With over 30+ service centers and more than 800 million payment cards personalized per year in 180+ countries, we deliver added-value card issuance services for issuers with clear benefits for the end users.

Limitless card personalization possibilities

At IDEMIA, we make sure that your card design, inserts and packaging are fully in line with the experience you want to provide to your customers. Unlimited design possibilities are offered to each individual consumer to make their banking cards truly unique. End-users can design their card, personalize it with a photo, engrave their signature and chose their PIN code. The level of card personalization is such that cardholders feel their card is designed just for them.

Instant card issuance and activation

Present all over the world, IDEMIA provides card issuance services that meet the needs of our fast-paced world. Physical and digital cards are issued and delivered swiftly. With Smart Instant Issuance, a consumer can walk out of a bank branch with an activated payment card in only eight minutes. And for those who choose to receive their new card directly at home, it will take no more than a few seconds to activate it and get their PIN on their bank app or via SMS.

Accelerated card issuance services for FinTechs

For FinTechs, we have created a turnkey solution, a package of card issuance services dedicated to their needs and agility. Our FinTech Accelerator Card Program enables a swift and global deployment for newcomers who want to transform the way we pay all over the word.

Payment card design and services to protect the planet

Going green is no longer optional and IDEMIA is ready to support banks in their ecological transformation with GREENPAY. In addition to providing recycled PVC card bodies, we offer a range of services, products and programs to help financial institutions reduce their carbon emissions: a full end-to-end green strategy to answer the expectations of environmentally conscious consumers.
