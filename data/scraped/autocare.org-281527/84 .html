However please note that if you do this you may not be able to use the full functionality of our Sites and it might impact your overall experience.

The three types of cookies we use are strictly necessary, analytics and performance, and advertising. Below are details about the cookies we’re using.

Strictly Necessary Cookies:

ASP.NET_SessionId: Used to maintain an anonymised user session by the server that tracks an active session. The cookie is deleted at the end of the session.

.ASPXAUTH: Indicates whether you have logged in.

PersonifyShoppingCartGUID: Stores your shopping cart content as anonymous user. Items can be transferred once user logs in.

EktGUID: A unique identifier for the current user is generated and assigned at the time they first arrive on the site.

Analytics and Performance Cookie (Google Analytics tracking includes, but is not limited to):

EkAnalytics: This cookie is associated with Ektron (Auto Care Association content management system), and is used to collect visitor information to enable performance measurement and improvement.

_ga: Google Analytics cookie is used to distinguish unique users by assigning a randomly generated number as a client identifier.

_utma: Google Analytics cookie used to distinguish users and sessions. The cookie is created when the javascript library executes and no existing __utma cookies exists. The cookie is updated every time data is sent to Google Analytics.

_utmz: Google Analytics cookie stores the traffic source or campaign that explains you reached our site. The cookie is created when the javascript library executes and is updated every time data is sent to Google Analytics.

feathr_session_id: This cookie allows to monitor how users move from one page within a website to another.
