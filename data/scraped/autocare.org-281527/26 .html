75th Annual AWDA Business Conference

Oct. 30 - 31, 2022 | Venetian Resort & Casino

who

The AWDA Conference is the largest Auto Care community conference and participation is available only to Auto Care Association member companies or shareholder distributor companies of an Auto Care member program group. Additional eligibility requirements are also noted below.

what

The AWDA Conference is centered around maintaining existing relationships between the premier vendors and distributors of the industry through a series of unique pre-scheduled one-on-one business meetings. Participants are top decision-makers allowing for AWDA’s One-on-Ones to serve as the most efficient way to conduct serious business during Industry Week.

when

The one-on-one’s will be held all day on Sunday Oct. 30 and Monday Oct. 31 prior to the start of AAPEX. The one-on-one meetings are held in Venetian guest suites which ensures confidentiality, comfortability, travel convenience, and most importantly, provides an environment that is conducive to the serious nature of discussions among the high-level attendees participating in these focused meetings.

Noted vendor requirements

AAPEX Booth Requirement: AWDA Vendor companies must reserve at least one (1) 10'x10' exhibit booth at AAPEX. AAPEX and AWDA have separate registration policies and fees. 
Venetian Guest Suite / One-on-One Meeting Room: Vendor companies must reserve one Venetian guest suite through the AWDA reservation link below for each registered meeting team. These suites may also be used as a sleeping room.
Auto Care Member with Existing Business Relationships: Vendor companies must be current members of the Auto Care Association and sell direct to at least seven distributor members or be approved by at least one major program group.

Process & Key Dates

The AWDA Conference registration process has a phased approach with particular deadlines where companies may expect the following:

Phase One: Companies commit and register their company/primary attendee. A designated individual must also be named to manage the scheduling process. 

Phase Two: Companies create accounts in the MeetMax scheduling software and select/rank their preferred companies. MeetMax will weigh each sides' rankings to prioritize meetings between companies with mutual, high ranking selections favoring the distributors' rankings.  

Phase Three: After automated schedules are shared, companies will have the ability to make manual edits, cancellations and/or request new meetings to possibly fill any vacant time slots.

    One-on-One Business Meetings
    • 21 Pre-Scheduled time slots
      • 10 slots on Sunday Oct. 30 and 11 slots on Monday Oct. 31.
      • First pre-scheduled time slot begins at 8:00 am each day. Last time slot begins at 4:15 pm on Sunday Oct 30 and 5:00 pm on Monday Oct. 31.
      • Each time slot is 40 minutes in length
      • 5 minutes of travel time between time slots
      • Ability to customize availability and "block" specific time slots
    • 2 Optional Self-Scheduled Time Slots
      • Each time slot is 30 minutes in length
      • Optional time slot is 7:15 am - 7:45 am on both days.
      • Opportunity to manually schedule these time slots will begin on Sept. 30, 2022
    • MeetMax Scheduling Software
      • Each company will create an account on the MeetMax Scheduling software.
      • Companies will select and rank the companies with which they would like to hold a meeting.
      • When creating meetings, MeetMax priorities mutual requests with high rankings, favoring the WDs rankings.
      • Companies may anonymously “opt-out” out meeting with certain companies and the system will block the creation of a meeting between those two companies. (Limit of 3 opt-outs)
      • Companies may bring multiple teams to allow for more one-on-one meetings.
        • There is no additional registration cost for added teams.
        • Teams that have a specific business function (Ex. Marketing, IT) may need to create their own MeetMax account to ensure distinct schedules
        • Companies bringing multiple teams just to allow for higher number of meetings can utilize just one MeetMax account.
