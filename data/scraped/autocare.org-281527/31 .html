International Matchmaking



Grow your business worldwide. Connecting U.S. companies and international partners/buyers.



June 6 – 10, 2022, virtual program

The Auto Care Association, in partnership with the United States Commercial Service (USCS), is excited to announce an International Matchmaking Virtual Program, connecting U.S. companies and international partners/buyers in the global automotive aftermarket industry.

U.S. companies interested in entering or increasing their presence in the international market have the opportunity to meet with pre-screened overseas prospects, distributors, buyers, importers, partners, and industry multipliers that have been recruited by the U.S. Embassy and U.S. Consulates around the globe.

Participants will have week-long access to 1-on-1 virtual meetings through a virtual program hosted by the U.S. Commercial Service and the Auto Care Association.

Eligibility: U.S. automotive aftermarket companies must promote export of products and services of at least 51% U.S. content by value to participate in this program.
