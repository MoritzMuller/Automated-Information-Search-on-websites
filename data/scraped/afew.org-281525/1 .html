Our Vision: a world where at-risk and underserved populations in society have access to health care without stigma and discrimination
Our Mission: Providing support and a strong international voice on rights and on health access to at –risk and underserved populations in the EECA Region
Our objective: GREATER VISIBILITY for underserved Populations Needs in EECA region
Our objective: STRENGTHENING PARTNERSHIP AND PARTNERS CAPACITIES on improving health and social protection for underserved populations & communities
Our objective: LINKING & LEARNING by providing platforms and opportunities for effective exchange and access to knowledge
