Donate to Friends of ELNET

Please use the form below to send your contribution online to Friends of ELNET. Friends of ELNET is a nonprofit organization that is tax-exempt under the United States Internal Revenue Code Section 501(c)(3). 

In its second decade of operations, ELNET continues to expand to a growing number of countries and fields. Each contribution, large or small, helps us deepen our reach and impact. We encourage you to contact us to discuss specific countries and projects that you’d like to support.

Support our work and join the Friends of ELNET Diplomacy Club which provides exclusive access to special programs and presents a unique platform to engage with top experts and policymakers on issues of key importance to Israel, and the global Jewish community. To learn more about the program levels and benefits, please contact lrosenblum@felnetus.org.

Donating by check? Tax-deductible donations can be made by check payable to:
Friends of ELNET
641 Lexington Avenue, 20th floor
New York, NY 10022

For more information about donating to Friends of ELNET, please contact rhalperin@felnetus.org.
