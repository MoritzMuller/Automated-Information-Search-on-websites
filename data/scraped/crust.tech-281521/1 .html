How You Can Use Corteza

Corteza lets you connect with any data source, allowing you to integrate and harmonize all your data, build, iterate and deploy unlimited record-based web apps or business workflows.

Connect

Connect and integrate with data from any machine-readable source, regardless of format.

Transform

Transform your data into a standardized JSON format that’s easy to work with and share.

Build

Create apps and workflows with visual, block-based tools and Javascript automations.

Deploy

Deploy or export apps and workflows instantly with one click and redeploy as often as you like.

More About the Corteza Platform

Rapid Application Development

Build unlimited native web apps fast without coding.

Use Corteza’s intuitive low-code tools to build, deploy and iterate limitless data-based web apps from the ground up, on top of, next to or to replace existing software and databases using powerful drag-and-drop tools.

More About App Development

Business Process Management

Design, automate, deploy, manage and modify your business processes.

Use Corteza’s visual workflow builder and scripting engine to easily create complex BPMN 2.0 workflows, scripting automations and enterprise-level ERP and business process management tools within and between applications.

More About Workflows

Integration Hub and Management

Connect with any data-based system and federate data out.

Use Corteza’s REST API and Integration Gateway to connect between and across environments, ecosystems, workflows and applications through a single unified platform. Or offer Corteza as an iPaaS by using it as middleware between third party applications.

More About Integrations

Customize Corteza Any Way You Want

Customize your Corteza UI, customize web app interfaces, create custom templates and more.

Basic Customization

Use built-in settings to customize Corteza to reflect your brand, including adding your logo, customizing the login screen, removing any obvious mentions of the name Corteza and so on.

Add CSS and Graphics

Add new CSS and graphical elements to the web interface to control how the Corteza UI looks, as well as create custom color schemes that match your brand.

Fork the Corteza Interface

Fork your Corteza user interfaces to add new block types to use with your data models or customize their every detail to match your brand while staying in sync with new Corteza releases.

Use Your Own Interface

Use your own or a third-party user interface or web portal that connects directly to and takes advantage of all the power of the Corteza backend server without using the Corteza interface at all.

About Planet Crust

Planet Crust is the creator of and driving force behind Corteza, which is used by over 12,000 organizations around the world. In addition to maintaining and developing Corteza, Planet Crust provides support, consulting, training and cloud-hosting services for Corteza.

Planet Crust serves enterprises, software providers, local governments and NGOs across the Globe in a wide variety of segments, including call centers, compliance, finance, healthcare, insurance, logistics, manufacturing, military, oil & gas, professional services, refugee aid, retail, telecommunication and more.

Planet Crust offers Corteza for free with the aim of helping organizations everywhere experience increased data interoperability and digital unity without loss of digital sovereignty.

Planet Crust Software Assurance and Support

Building complex applications and workflows on Corteza is fast, but requires skill and know-how. Planet Crust Software Assurance and Support covers all aspects of your work with Corteza so you can resolve your issues quickly and get on with your work.

Resolve Issues Quickly

We’ll train your team in Corteza best practices, advise you on your set-ups, help you resolve complex configuration problems and prioritize your bug fixes so you create the solutions you need.

Speed Up Innovation

Our detailed onboarding process documents all your goals and requirements so we can guide you through the app-building cycle and help you maintain your apps.

Reduce Operational Risk

We handle every aspect of maintaining the code and supporting, improving and iterating the platform. And if there’s a feature you want, simply request to put it on our roadmap.

Future-Proof Corteza

Help us maintain and develop the platform long into the future, plus get an integral say in determining our roadmap and where Corteza will go over time so it matches your needs.
